Leaving for Canada

If you're an international traveller, make sure you've downloaded and filled out ArriveCAN before flying by visiting http://www.canada.ca/en/border/border-services-agency/arrivecan.html. Make sure you fill out Advance Declaration after submitting your ArriveCAN information. To do so, just follow the prompt on your ArriveCAN receipt page.

Upon your arrival it's important to remember that some health checks remain: Customs officials will verify your ArriveCAN compliance. Partially vaccinatedor unvaccinated individuals must quarantine for 14 days.
